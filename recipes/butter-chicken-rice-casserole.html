Butter Chicken Rice Casserole

This butter chicken rice casserole is very easy to put together and is on the table in about 40 minutes," says Allrecipes recipe developer Jaelyn Luong. "Serve with a salad or simple vegetable side for a great weeknight family dinner.

Prep Time:
10 mins

Cook Time:
30 mins

Total Time:
40 mins

Servings:
6

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) jar butter chicken sauce
  • 3/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup basmati rice, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • cilantro, for garnish
  • lime wedges, for garnish

Direction

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine the butter chicken sauce and broth in a large oven-safe skillet. Bring to boiling over medium-high heat.
  3. Stir in the chicken, rice, onion, and salt until combined. Bring to a simmer and remove from heat.
  4. Cover the skillet tightly with the lid or foil and bake until chicken and rice are cooked through, for 25 to 30 minutes.
  5. Let stand for 15 minutes. Fluff the rice before serving. Top with cilantro and serve with lime wedges.
